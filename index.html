The Association of Software Engineering Students (ASES), Federal University of Technology, Owerri, is the student-run organization under the Software Engineering Department, fostering professional development, peer interaction, and practical skills among members..

Join the Family Our achievements

Driven by Students, for Students

ASES FUTO is led by a dedicated executive council of students who are passionate about advancing the association's mission.

Collaboration:

We believe that the best solutions are built together. ASES provides a platform for students to collaborate on projects, share knowledge, and support one another in an inclusive environment.

Innovation:

We challenge our members to push boundaries and turn creative ideas into tangible solutions. Our events, like the Web Design Workshop, are designed to spark innovation and showcase student talent.

Networking Events:

ASES FUTO provides opportunities for students to connect and network with other tech enthusiasts and developers by hosting tech events that bring then together.

Skill development:

The association provides resources and opportunities for members to gain practical exposure to software development and problem-solving techniques.

Social events:

The Director of Socials organizes parties and other gatherings to foster community and build relationships among software engineering students while keeping them entertained.

Leadership:

We encourage students to become leaders by encouraging them to become the best version of themselves and awarding them for selfless and honorable acts.
